Privacy Policy:

The app does not collect, use, store, transfer, handle or share user or device information in any way or form. It does not automatically communicate nor does it have the functionality to communicate externally with any party, whether through the internet or any other medium. The app is purposely designed to operate and function offline, to create and modify text files which are stored locally on the device storage, and which are only possible to transfer manually by the user. The code base of the app is open sourced and everyone is invited and encouraged to inspect it.
